SEO

What is Search Engine Optimization or SEO? Search engines such as Google or DuckDuckGo crawl the web and index as many websites as they can. Using a proprietary secret algorythm they rate webpages using a varity of critrea. Why does this matter for your website? Well ask yourself, how many times have you gone to the second page of a google search? If you're like me, the answer is somewhere around "never," which means it is incredibly important that your page is in the first few results of any search engine. Using SEO best practises on your website means you are more likely to be at the top of any search-engines results.
